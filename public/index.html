Spin Mechanics

Pool-based
  • 1 Lot = 1 Spin Credit (1 USD equivalent).
  • Spin x10 can trigger a bonus (example: +1 bonus spin or a probability boost).
  • Free Spins (marketing) are in a separate wallet and do not affect Sure Win or Lambo Fund.
  • Fairness: winner selection is server-side, seed-based, auditable.

Sure Win

Guaranteed
  • Users accumulate Sure Win Points from eligible activity.
  • Points unlock guaranteed rewards at milestones (e.g., 500, 1000, 1500).
  • Progress bar shows: current points, next milestone, points remaining.
  • Unlocks are idempotent and cannot duplicate.

Lambo Fund

Milestone
  • Lambo Fund receives 1 USD per traded lot (from the 2 USD cap per lot).
  • Track progress for Client, IB, MIB levels.
  • Dashboard shows self contribution and network contribution (for IB/MIB).

Expiry & Notifications

Time-sensitive
  • Spin credits can be configured to expire (e.g., monthly).
  • Expiry monitor shows how many spins expire and in how many days.
  • Notifications: expiry alerts, milestone proximity, jackpot pool updates.
  • Anti-spam rule: max 2 notifications/day.
Financial safety principle: keep a hard cap on company exposure (example: 2 USD per lot). Monitor pool balances, pending rewards liability, and risk thresholds (example: alert if liability ratio exceeds 70%).
